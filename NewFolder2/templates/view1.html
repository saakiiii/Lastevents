Lesson from the book of history

by - averin|30-06-2022Report this article

    In my school days, I used to sleep when my teacher took history class. Indeed, history is one of the interesting subjects to learn. It is the best teacher. One can learn how to live a life from the past. I read many history books like Indian Mughals history, Independence history of India, and history of world wars.

    It gives us the knowledge of how they lived, what was their culture, how they learned etc. As you come to taste the history you will build yourself as a good human from the lessons of our ancestors. History makes you understand the world. You will start to think in a unique way


    We can learn about the political systems, how the society was organized, and how the community reached its maximum strength. We can avoid certain mistakes which were done by the people who lived before. You might find that the power of a country or a kingdom depends on military power. We can find more inspiring personalities from history.

      Usually, an autobiography is a memory of the writer, it is full of experiences, you can learn from them and it is easy to find solutions to your problems.
